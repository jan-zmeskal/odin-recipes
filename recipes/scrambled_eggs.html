Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is a delicious, easy and quick meal that's also very nutritions at the same time.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Eggs
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Cut the onion into small pieces
  2. Pour some oil on a pan an heat it up
  3. In the meantime, break the eggs and mix them in a bowl
  4. Mix bit of salt and pepper with the eggs
  5. Put the mixture on a pan and cook it
  6. Eat with bread
